# 01 — Business Brief

## Company Context

**NexaPay** is a digital credit-card issuer operating under **Nexantara Financial**, serving retail cardholders across Indonesia through fully-digital onboarding and transactions. The portfolio spans four card brands — Mastercard, Visa, American Express and JCB — analysed over a six-month non-fraud transaction window (L6M).

- Active cards (cleaned): **5,528**
- Cardholders: **1,938**
- Card brands: **4**
- Data window: **Last 6 Months (L6M)**

## Problem Statement

NexaPay lacks visibility into where profit leaks to fraud and which cardholders are under-transacting, making it impossible to prioritize growth campaigns or fraud controls. Without structured segmentation, warm-but-low-frequency cardholders quietly slip toward dormancy, while card brands and credit segments are treated uniformly with no data-driven targeting.

## Goal of the Analysis

> Maximize business growth by identifying high-value transaction patterns, minimizing losses from fraud, and developing cardholder profiles to drive greater credit-card usage within the next 6 months.

## SMART Objective

To quantify NexaPay's fraud impact on profit, segment 1,938 cardholders by RFM behaviour, and deliver data-driven recommendations targeting a **15–25% transaction-frequency uplift** among priority segments within the next 6 months.

## Analysis Objectives

1. **Profit & Fraud** — Quantify net card profit and the fraud impact over the L6M window; benchmark the fraud rate against the industry.
2. **Cardholder Segmentation** — Apply RFM to segment 1,938 cardholders; flag priority segments that are warm but under-transacting.
3. **Growth Strategy** — Translate behaviour and segments into a concrete 15–25% frequency-uplift plan by brand, age and credit profile.

## Success Metric

A prioritized, evidence-backed action plan (usage incentives, loyalty program, real-time fraud control) targeting a measurable **15–25% frequency uplift** among the 457 priority cardholders, achieved more efficiently than new-customer acquisition.
