# 06 — Recommendations & Action Plan

Three data-driven moves target a **15–25% transaction-frequency uplift** among the 457 priority cardholders while protecting the ~14–15% of profit currently eroded by fraud.

---

## Recommendation 1 — Card Usage Incentives

**Finding:** 457 priority cardholders (Potential Loyalist + Customers Needing Attention) are warm but under-transacting — many stop at 1–2 transactions.

**Why it happens**
- No repeat-usage nudges after the first transactions
- Cardholders not enrolled in any loyalty mechanic
- No milestone that rewards the next transaction

**Action**
- **Tiered milestone incentives** — cashback / fee waivers tied to the 3rd, 5th and 8th transaction (light on margin)
- **Personalised nudges** — in-app, WhatsApp and email reminders based on past spend categories (dining, travel, top-ups)

**Expected impact:** Increase transaction frequency by **15–25%** among targeted segments, at lower cost than new-customer acquisition.

---

## Recommendation 2 — Loyalty / Membership Program

**Finding:** Champions (398) and Loyal Customers (287) drive most revenue, but there is no structured mechanic to protect them or pull warm segments upward.

**Why it matters**
- High-value cardholders can drift to lower-frequency segments unnoticed
- Warm segments lack a reason to consolidate spend on the card
- Retention is cheaper than reactivation

**Action**
- **Points / tiered membership** — reward repeat card usage; unlock benefits at usage tiers to lift frequency
- **Retention guardrails** — alert when a Loyal/Champion cardholder's recency slips; trigger targeted re-engagement

**Expected impact:** Protect the revenue concentration in top segments and convert Potential Loyalists into repeat users.

---

## Recommendation 3 — Real-Time Fraud Management

**Finding:** Fraud rate is low (0.22%) yet still erodes ~14–15% of card profit — losses are reviewed after the fact, not intercepted.

**Root-cause hypotheses**
- High-volume brands (Mastercard, Visa) concentrate fraud exposure
- Amex carries higher impact per fraudulent transaction
- No step-up authentication targeted at high-risk transactions

**Action**
- **Real-time monitoring** — score transactions live; flag high-risk channel / merchant / method patterns
- **Targeted step-up OTP** — apply OTP only to high-risk transactions, cutting losses without adding friction for everyone

**Expected impact:** Reduce fraud-driven profit erosion while preserving a frictionless experience for low-risk transactions.

---

## 6-Month Implementation Roadmap

| Phase | Timeline | Actions |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Implement** | Month 1–2 | Launch tiered usage incentives (3rd/5th/8th txn) · stand up real-time fraud + step-up OTP · enrol priority segments into loyalty pilot |
| **Monitor** | Month 3–4 | Track frequency uplift by segment & brand · refresh RFM monthly · watch fraud-loss ratio vs baseline |
| **Evaluate** | Month 5–6 | Measure uplift vs 15–25% target · tune incentive tiers & fraud guardrails · report ROI to the business |
